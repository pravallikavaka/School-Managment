About Why Choose Us

We understand that making decisions for your child’s education can be challenging. Henderson International School is proud to offer an unparalleled educational experience that will inspire your child to reach their full life potential.Our school is the fruit of our sincere and relentless efforts to create an educational space that can augment a child’s interest in academics. The campus is housed in a picturesque location to bring about a perfect balance between mind, body, soul, and connection to nature. It has spacious and airy classrooms, indoor and outdoor play areas, which are designed to align with world-class standards in education. This conducive learning space that we offer has played a vital part in nurturing a community of modern learners who excel in their own unique fields of interest today.
